Why Sell to us?

At Stonebridge Holdings LLC, we make the transition process smooth, respectful, and valuable — for both the owner and the legacy you've built.

We Respect Your Legacy

We don't flip businesses. We nurture them. Our mission is to grow and protect the foundation you've worked hard to build — for your team, your customers, and your community.

Flexible, Thoughtful Transitions

Whether you're ready to exit fully or prefer to stay involved during a handoff period, we work around your timeline — not ours.

No Corporate Red Tape

We're not a giant fund. We're a nimble, hands-on firm that makes decisions quickly and transparently. You'll always deal directly with the buyer.

Your People Matter

We believe a business is only as good as its team. We prioritize keeping your staff, culture, and operations intact — and helping them grow.

Creative Deal Structures

We tailor every acquisition to meet both parties' goals. That might mean seller financing, equity rollover, or tax-conscious exit terms — whatever serves you best.

Our Ideal acquisition

We're looking for service-based businesses with:

  • $1.5M–$5M in annual revenue
  • $500K–$1.5M+ in owner earnings (SDE/EBITDA)
  • A stable team or manager in place
  • Owners ready for a professional, respectful exit

If that sounds like you — we'd love to talk.
