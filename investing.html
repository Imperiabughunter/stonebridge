Why Partner/Invest With Us?

At Stonebridge Holdings, we specialize in acquiring, building, and scaling small businesses with stable cash flow and strong fundamentals. Our focus is on long-term value creation—not speculation.

We offer investors the opportunity to:

  • Participate in Profitable Acquisitions: We target businesses with $500K+ in owner earnings (SDE) and a history of stability, giving you access to real, cash-flowing investments.

  • Receive Preferred Returns: Our investors receive preferred returns before any profit is distributed to us, aligning our success with yours.

  • Maintain Downside Protection: Through thoughtful deal structures—including seller financing and standby notes—we reduce risk and increase capital efficiency.

  • Stay Hands-Off: We manage the day-to-day operations and value creation. You get the benefits of ownership without the burden of management.

  • Invest in Real Assets with Real Returns: These are not ideas or pre-revenue startups. These are tangible businesses with existing teams, customers, and cash flow.

We're not just raising capital—we're building partnerships. Let's grow wealth together.
