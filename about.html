Meet the Founder – Christian Saletri

Hi, I'm Christian Saletri, the Founder and CEO of Stonebridge Holdings LLC.

With over 6 years of accounting experience, I've helped businesses clean up their books, optimize their operations, and make smarter financial decisions. I've seen firsthand how strong companies can lose momentum when systems don't scale—or when owners are stretched too thin.

That's why I started Stonebridge Holdings. My goal is to acquire recession-resistant, service-based businesses and give them the tools, leadership, and systems they need to grow—especially by leveraging AI to reduce expenses and boost efficiency.

I'm especially focused on:

  • Helping founders transition smoothly while preserving their legacy
  • Keeping teams and company culture intact
  • Installing scalable systems that reduce owner-dependency
  • Delivering long-term value for investors, customers, and employees

Outside of business, I'm passionate about fitness, freedom, and helping others succeed—which is why I'm building Stonebridge to be a company that adds real value to every business we touch.

If you're a business owner looking to sell, an investor interested in partnerships, or someone who just wants to connect—I'd love to hear from you.
